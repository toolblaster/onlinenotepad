Free Online Notepad & Rich Text Editor

Welcome to Online Notepad, your free, secure, and easy-to-use web-based text editor. Whether you need to jot down quick thoughts, draft a blog post, or manage your daily tasks, our tool offers a distraction-free environment with powerful rich text formatting capabilities.

Key Features

  • Rich Text Formatting: Customize your notes with bold, italics, lists, headings, and more.
  • Privacy First: Your notes are stored locally in your browser. We do not track your content or store it on our servers.
  • Dark Mode: Reduce eye strain with a built-in dark theme, perfect for late-night writing.
  • Auto-Save: Never lose your work again. Your text is saved automatically as you type.
  • Export & Share: Download your notes as text files or create a shareable link instantly.

How to Use

Simply start typing in the main editor area. Use the toolbar at the top to format your text. Your progress is saved automatically to your browser's local storage, so you can close the tab and come back later to pick up right where you left off.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?
Yes, Online Notepad is completely free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

Do I need to create an account?
No, you can start writing immediately without signing up.
